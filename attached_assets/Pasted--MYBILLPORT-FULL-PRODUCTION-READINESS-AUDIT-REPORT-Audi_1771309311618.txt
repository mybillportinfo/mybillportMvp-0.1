Findings:

· Recurring Cloud Function: Runs daily. Good. Must ensure it doesn't exceed Firestore read/write quotas.
· Re-renders: No obvious infinite loops. useEffect dependencies look correct.

Issues Found:

Verdict: ⚠ Dashboard pagination needed before scaling to heavy users.
