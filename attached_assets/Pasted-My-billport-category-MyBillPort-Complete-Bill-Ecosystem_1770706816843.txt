MyBillPort – Complete Bill Ecosystem Document (Canada)

1. Purpose of This Document

This document defines every major bill type a Canadian resident may need to track, manage, and eventually pay inside MyBillPort.

Scope:
	•	Personal (not business)
	•	Canada-wide
	•	Covers utilities, telecom, finance, housing, insurance, subscriptions, and government-related bills
	•	Vendor-agnostic (supports current + future APIs)

This is not about implementation yet — it’s about what must exist.

⸻

3. Utilities

3.1 Electricity

Who issues it
	•	Provincial utilities
	•	Municipal utilities

Major Providers (Examples)
	•	Ontario: Hydro One, Toronto Hydro, Hydro Ottawa
	•	Quebec: Hydro-Québec
	•	BC: BC Hydro
	•	Alberta: ENMAX, EPCOR
	•	Manitoba: Manitoba Hydro
	•	Saskatchewan: SaskPower
	•	Atlantic: NB Power, Nova Scotia Power

Payment cycle
	•	Monthly or bi-monthly

Required fields
	•	Utility provider name
	•	Account number
	•	Billing period
	•	Due date
	•	Amount due
	•	Last payment date
	•	Metered usage (optional, future)

13. Core Functional Requirements (Non-Negotiable)

For every bill, MyBillPort must support:
	1.	Add bill manually
	2.	Edit bill
	3.	Delete bill
	4.	Due-date tracking
	5.	Overdue detection
	6.	In-app notifications
	7.	Free tier limit (e.g., 3 bills)
	8.	Paid tier unlock (future)
